Five Largest Protests in U.S. History

Listed below are the top five most attended protests in the country's history:

  1. George Floyd protests nationwide with up to 26 million protestors.
  2. Earth Day protests in 1970 across the country drew about 20 million protestors.
  3. The 2017 Women's March had about 5.6 million people nationwide.
  4. The March for Our Lives against gun control in 2018 drew about 2 million protestors.
  5. The 2018 Women's March drew about 1.5 million people across the country.
